## Research limitations

- Public web pages can change frequently; URL contents may shift after the date of research.
- Parsed HTML or text from search tools is not equivalent to a full source-code audit.
- Some decisions require hands-on prototyping rather than survey research, especially primitive choice (Lit vs Stencil), Web Component SSR feasibility, and update / codemod design.
- Legal compliance (508, ADA, EAA) requires counsel and formal accessibility review; this package provides product and technical strategy, not legal advice.
- Reddit / HackerNews / general developer-community evidence on USWDS pain points is sparse — most signal comes from GitHub Discussions and contractor blog posts.
