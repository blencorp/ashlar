# Code of Conduct

Ashlar follows a simple standard: participate professionally, assume public-service impact, and keep criticism focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Welcome contributors across agencies, vendors, civic-tech groups, and regulated industries.
- Ground technical claims in evidence.
- Treat accessibility, security, and public-service usability as first-order concerns.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing governance, accessibility, or security discussions.
- Implying official endorsement where none exists.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, moderate, temporarily restrict, or ban participants who violate this code. Security or safety issues can be escalated privately through the security policy.

Reports can be sent to security@blencorp.com until a dedicated community conduct contact is established.
